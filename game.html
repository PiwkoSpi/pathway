About the Game

The once-thriving Kingdom of Eryndor has fallen into despair. Beneath its surface lies an expansive underground realm, where countless villagers and soldiers have been imprisoned by the tyrannical Dragon Vezareth. This fearsome creature guards the precious Crystal of Liberation, a magical artifact that can undo its dark curse and free the captives. The knight, Sir Aldric, must embark on a perilous journey to rescue the people and restore hope to the kingdom.
